What is Motivation exactly?
According to the people of VeryWellMind.com: "Motivation is the process that initiates, guides, and maintains goal-oriented behaviors. It is what causes you to act, whether it is getting a glass of water to reduce thirst or reading a book to gain knowledge." In my eyes this is a very good explanation of what Motivation is, and what it means to is "It's the driving force behind human actions!"
