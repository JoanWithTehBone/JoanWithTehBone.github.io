Programming Experience

So especially for you, reader, I have gathered some screenshots from my older site, which I reworked for the biggest part by hand.

Secondly, the lack of self-esteem in my coding has disappeared. I used to copy paste a lot of code without having the knowledge of using it, which is exactly what SHOULDN'T be done.


Last but not least, my motivation and joy I find in coding nowadays has increased. Even though I don't have the freetime to do so, I can at least show you that I'm passionate about working.
